## Example runcards 
This folder contains the runcards used to produce the results in the paper "New Physics Reach through Precision at Future Colliders:
a Multi-Pronged Approach", arXiv:2604.xxyy, by Tommasso Armadillo, Eugenia Celada, Jaco ter Hoeve, Fabio Maltoni, Luca Mantani, Juan Rojo, Alejo N. Rossia, Simone Tentori, Marion O. A. Thomas, and Eleni Vryonidou.
